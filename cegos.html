About Trudi

Trudi Chong is a facilitator, executive coach, and L&D strategist who has spent 20+ years helping organisations build the leadership and communication capability they actually need — not the programmes they think they need. She combines deep facilitation craft with real business credibility, having built L&D functions from scratch at Airbnb, Meta, and GovTech. She is direct, engaging, and obsessively practical.
