The Grant
What EWTP actually covers

EWTP stands for the Enterprise Workforce Transformation Package. It is Singapore's newest and most generous workforce grant, launched in March 2026 by WSG, MOM, and SSG.

It has three components that can be stacked — meaning the right programme can unlock up to S$150,000 per company, with the government covering up to 70% of costs.

Your company likely qualifies if you
Are registered and operating in Singapore
Have at least 3 local (SC or PR) employees
Have not yet signed with a consultant for this scope
Want to genuinely transform how your team works

Assess needs, redesign roles, build strategy
S$50K
Component 2 — Capability Building
Train managers, build leadership capability
S$60K
Component 3 — Workforce Tech

S$90K
Maximum total per company
S$150,000
