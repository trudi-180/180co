The Real Problem

Governance doesn't fail on paper.

In family offices, foundations, and social impact organisations, the frameworks are usually sound. The strategies are well-intentioned. The mission is clear.

What breaks down is the human layer — how boards communicate with management, how multi-generational stakeholders navigate differing values, how passionate teams align without burning out, how leaders transition gracefully without taking the organisation down with them.

180co. works on the human layer. We build the leadership capability, communication culture, and team coherence that allows your mission to actually succeed.

Where We Help

Three challenges we hear most often

01

Stakeholder Communication Breakdowns

Boards and management talk past each other. Family members and professional trustees operate on different assumptions. Grantors and grantees struggle to align. Relationships fray even when everyone means well.

→ Leadership Coaching + Facilitation
02

Leadership Transitions Without a Net

Next-generation family members stepping into leadership roles. Executive directors inheriting legacy cultures. Professionals moving from corporate to purpose-driven contexts. Transitions managed badly derail organisations.

→ Young Leader Accelerator + Coaching
03

High-Passion, Low-Cohesion Teams

Mission-driven teams attract brilliant, committed people who often work in silos. Decision-making is slow. Accountability is murky. The founder or ED becomes the bottleneck. Burnout is common.

→ Team Alignment Workshop + Systems
What We Do

Three ways to work together

01

Leadership Coaching

Individual · 3–6 Months

ICF-methodology coaching for leaders navigating transitions, stakeholder complexity, or performance challenges. Bi-weekly sessions plus ad-hoc support for high-stakes conversations.

Best for next-generation leaders, executive directors, and family office principals who want a trusted thinking partner.
02

Team Alignment Workshop

Group · 1–2 Days

Custom-designed facilitation for boards, management teams, or cross-generational groups. Focus on how we communicate, how we make decisions, and how we hold each other accountable.

Best for boards, leadership teams, and multi-stakeholder groups who need to find shared ground and work more effectively together.
03

Transformation Sprint

Organisation · 90 Days

An embedded engagement where 180co. partners deeply with your organisation — assessing needs, designing interventions, building capability, and creating systems that last beyond the engagement.

Best for organisations in active transition who need more than a workshop — a trusted partner who understands context and delivers change.
